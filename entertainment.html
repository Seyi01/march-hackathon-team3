Toyin Abraham’s new film Ige to premiere on April 3


Toyin Abraham’s upcoming film, Ige – The Unlikely Oil Merchant- has been slated for a premiere at IMAX Cinemas, Lekki, on April 3. Directed by Biodun Stephen, ‘Ige’ tells the grass to grace story of Nigerian oil magnate Bashir Fakorede who rose to the top of his chosen field against all odds.

“I’ll be praying for you” – Mercy Eke reacts to troll who accused her of trying to portray relevance


Reality star, Mercy Eke has voluntarily offered to pray for a social media user who accused her of trying to show how relevant she has been ever since she won the Big Brother Naija show (Season 4).

“Nigerian cinema will soon be turned to Youtube” – Actress, Yvonne Jegede blast colleagues over hasty movie production

Nollywood actress, Yvonne Jegede has slammed her colleagues in the movie industry following the rush and haste applied in the process of movie production/making. In her words: “Today they are on set shooting, and next minute they have a cinema date, next tomorrow the movie don enter cinema. Them go soon turn Nigerian cinema to YouTube.”
